# Company Evaluation

Systematic company research and comparison methodology.

## Purpose

This skill ensures thorough, consistent company research before investing interview energy. It produces comparable evaluation documents for decision-making.

## Research Sources

**Must-check:**
- Glassdoor (overall + engineering)
- levels.fyi (compensation)
- LinkedIn (team/leadership)
- Crunchbase (funding/stability)

**Should-check:**
- Recent news (funding, layoffs)
- Blog/engineering posts (culture)
- Blind (unfiltered sentiment)

## Output

Creates structured company files in `job_search/companies/[Company].md` with:
- Quick stats and metrics
- Mission and product assessment
- Role details and requirements
- Compensation analysis
- Culture assessment
- Technical evaluation
- Decision criteria scoring
- Interview tracking

## Usage

**Invoke:** `/company-evaluation`

**Common commands:**
- "Research [Company]" - Full evaluation
- "Quick screen [Company]" - 10-minute viability check
- "Compare [A] vs [B]" - Side-by-side analysis

## Key Questions

**Always answer:**
- Does comp meet $225K floor?
- Are there toxic management signals?
- Is the pace sustainable?
- Will Shabbat be respected?
- Is there path to Principal?

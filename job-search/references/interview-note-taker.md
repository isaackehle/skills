# Interview Note Taker

Capture factual, structured notes without over-processing them in real time.

## Note-taking markers
- `Q:` question asked to me
- `A:` my answer summary
- `IQ:` question I asked interviewer
- `IR:` interviewer response
- `RF:` red flag
- `GF:` green flag
- `FU:` follow-up needed
- `?` unclear / verify later
- `!` important moment

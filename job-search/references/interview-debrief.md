# Interview Debrief

Run after an interview when live notes already exist. Base conclusions on notes, not memory or vibes alone.

## Debrief Structure

Add to the bottom of the live notes file, or create a separate `debrief-[Stage]-[Date].md` in `companies/[Company]/interviews/`.

```markdown
## Debrief — [Company] / [Stage] / [Date]

### What Went Well

-

### What Was Hard

-

### Questions They Asked Me

1.

### Questions I Asked Them

1.

### Key Takeaways

-

### Red Flags Identified

-

### Green Flags Identified

-

### Unanswered Questions

-

### Glassdoor Validation

- Were the Glassdoor concerns validated or contradicted by this call?

### Energy Check

- [ ] Energized and excited
- [ ] Neutral / professional
- [ ] Drained or uncomfortable
- [ ] Body signals (anxiety, shutdown, people-pleasing mode)

### Decision

- [ ] ✅ Advance — I want to continue
- [ ] ⏸️ Pause — need more information before deciding
- [ ] ❌ Withdraw — not the right fit
- [ ] 🤔 Unclear — need to process more

**Reasoning:**

### Next Steps

- [ ] Send thank-you email (within 24 hours)
- [ ] Follow up on: [specific open item]
- [ ] Prepare for: [next stage]
- [ ] Research: [specific area uncovered]
```

## Debrief Rules

- Separate facts, interpretations, and open questions — label them clearly
- Include energy and nervous system signals as subjective observations, not weaknesses
- If the decision is Advance but energy was Drained — flag the contradiction explicitly
- Recommend: advance, pause, withdraw, or unclear — never leave it open-ended
- Update the position file and job matrix with the new status after debrief

## Thank-You Email

Send within 24 hours of each stage. Keep it brief.

```text
Subject: Thank you — [Your Name] — [Role at Company]

Hi [Name],

Thank you for the time today. I particularly appreciated [one specific thing from the conversation].

[Optional: one sentence of genuine follow-through or question.]

Looking forward to [next steps].

Best,
[Your Name]
```

Do not copy-paste a generic template verbatim — personalize the middle line to something real from the call.
